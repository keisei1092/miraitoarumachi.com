ミライトアルマチ構想

サークル名は
”未来と或る街”10年後，100年後，それ以上の未来の情景
”未来と在る町”未来を見据え直向きに生きる私たちの感情や風景
という二つの側面を併せ持つ創作を目標に名付けられる．

Circle name "Mirai To Aru Machi" means
"Mirai (in future) Toaru (A certain) Machi (city)": Cities of the future. Thinking about the sceneries of the future
"Mirai (future) to Aru (with) Machi (town)": Towns with the future. Describing our lives of today and nature
We want to add such two features to my creation, I named the circle name.
